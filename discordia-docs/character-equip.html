!Equip

!Equip weapon_name

The !Equip command allows a character to equip a weapon that is in their inventory.

Note: You must be logged in, have an active character selected and have the target weapon in the active character's inventory.

~ Example ~

!Equip BigOldStick

This active character will now face combat with the weapon called the "BigOldStick", however that item is listed in their inventory.
